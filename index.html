Hypothesis
My project examines global CO₂ emissions from 1855 to 1999. The goal is to understand how carbon output changed over time and what major events may have influenced these changes. My research question asks: How did global CO₂ emissions rise across this period, and what historical patterns appear in the data? ...
My hypothesis is that CO₂ emissions increased the fastest during periods of rapid industrial growth, especially after World War II, and slowed during major economic downturns or world conflicts. By analyzing this long-term dataset, I hope to reveal how industrial development and global events shaped modern climate impact.
